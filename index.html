The REAL-TOK ecosystem: Phase 1
We are paving the future of the real estate sector by introducing a variety of solutions for financing, token-based incentive programs for loyal customers and transactional efficiency.

  • Real estate token

    The RLTO token is used to facilitate payments for broker fees and properties at discounted rates compared to traditional fiat payments. The token also essentially represents the company’s highly valued portfolio of sustainable real estate projects.

  • Experienced company

    The company has already built a portfolio of more than 500 flats across various locations in Vienna. Most of these projects are in their final stage of construction and approval, translating into much lower risk for early investors due to the expected high evaluation of the company’s portfolio.

  • Token growth and buyback

    The REALTO Group is convinced of the success of the token and its increase in value. The company commits to it's early investors an optional buyback after seven years at the main sale price of 1,00€, if the value increase expectations are not met.

The REAL-TOK ecosystem: Phase 2
In Stage 2 of the REAL-TOK ecosystem development, we will expand the already existing scope of services available in Stage 1 by creating an advanced digital platform. The platform will introduce a variety of additional services for all stakeholders in the real estate sector, which are currently unique to the cryptocurrency and real estate markets.
  • Decentralized marketplace
    We are planning on introducing the world’s first decentralized marketplace for properties that allows direct peer-to-peer interaction between prospective homeowners and property sellers.
  • Digital contracting
    We will also offer the option for digital contracting, allowing parties to close fully immutable contracts for property purchases on the blockchain.
  • Real estate management tools
    We will offer a complex set of different management tools for real estate companies such as brokers and even building management companies.
  • Rent payments
    We will offer the possibility for tenants to execute rent payments to properties listed by REAL-TOK in RLTO tokens at discounted prices through the dashboard.
  • Informational resources
    All users will have free access to informational resources about real estate that will vary from industry analysis to recommendations about buying and selling.
  • Advertising
    We will allow stakeholders such as real estate agencies to deliver ads on the digital platform in order to reach a broader spectrum of potential property buyers.
