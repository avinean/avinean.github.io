By ARULSINGAM INVESTMENTS LTD
The founding company ARULSINGAM INVESTMENTS Ltd is located in the UK with currently two major projects that are strongly interlinked – Project ARSI and ARSI Charity. Thanks to our previous business endeavors, we can rely on an already existing international community from England, Germany, Sri Lanka, Denmark, India and the Netherlands. We are combining a decade of experience in real estate with the latest blockchain technological advancements, offering lower transaction costs and payment timeframes than any other existing solution in the current real estate market.
Meet Arsi
ARSI is a BSC token that offers a multitude of various use cases for the real estate sector and its community. Starting with rental payments in crypto that come with a discount compared to traditional FIAT rents, ARSI offers all sorts of benefits surrounding the growing real estate portfolio of ARULSINGAM INVESTEMENTS LTD. Supported by this large portfolio of real estate projects, the Arsi token adopts an ingenious tokenomics structure that will nurture its use cases in the long-term and establish it as the utility token for crypto and real estate enthusiasts.
Arsi Charity
Investors will benefit from the positive development of ARSI value over time and we will ensure that a significant amount of ARSI will benefit charitable projects to support people in need and tackling current issues that we consider significant like creating a steady supply of drinking water, access to education, reducing CO2 consumption, fighting poverty and famine.

Incentives
We want to give continuous loyalty benefits to the users who are using the ARSI token. First and foremost, this will apply to tenants with our own real estate portfolio, but we plan to extend the benefits to all ARSI holders with the growth of the ecosystem.
The possibilities are reaching from a monthly discount on an ARSI users rent in one of our apartments to a discount for a holiday home or short-term rental office or apartment in one of Europe’s major cities.
The ARSI NFT
Collection
We are launching a limited portfolio of ARSI-related Non-Fungible-Tokens (NFTs) inspired by our own tech-lion that is the symbol of our project. The proceedings collected via the sale of our NFTs will provide additional funding and liquidity.

The NFTs are always based on four base pictures that become a set of 25 NFTs per picture and build up to seasons that end in a collection of 2.500 NFTs. In total only four collections will be available, making it a limited portfolio of 10.000 NFTs.

The rollout of additional sets and collections of NFTs with various themes such as virtual reality, major cities, gaming and countries, inspired by our company and its values will be exectued by our talented design team in cooperation with external partners and artists

The first set of 25 NFT is already created and launched - youcan find them on opensea.io/Arulsingam-ARSI
